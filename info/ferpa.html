The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) set forth requirements regarding the privacy of student records. FERPA regulations allow you, the student, to have some control over who is allowed to have access to your school records and personal information. For further information about FERPA, please see the Liberty University FERPA Policy and Release Infomation The following page can be used to set up a Personal Identification Number(PIN) for anyone you choose to be granted access to your private student information. Either individual PINs can be created for each category, or the "All 3 FERPA Categories" category can be used to create a single PIN to allow each category of your information to be shared. To increase security, you may change or remove your PIN at any time. Liberty University representatives will not release any private information about you unless the correct PIN(s) is (are) given by the requestor.
